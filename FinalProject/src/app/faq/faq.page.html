FAQ Page

Frequently Ask Question
How to change the theme to the dark mode?
 To chenge to the dark theme, just click the "Dark Mode" button in the homepage.
How to edit and delete the class, task or mark?
 To delete the list, just slide the list to the left and click the "trash bin" icon. To edit the list, click the "create" icon and rewrite the details then click "update" button.
How to mark the task as completed?
 To mark the completed task, just click the list of tasks that you want to mark as completed.
How to add the class, task and mark?
 To add the lists, just click the "+" button then fill in the details and click the "cloud" button at the right top of the page to save it.
